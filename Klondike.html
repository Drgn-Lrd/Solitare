How to Play

Klondike Solitaire
  • Drag a card (or a valid stack) onto a foundation or another tableau pile.
  • Double-tap a card to send it straight to its foundation if possible.
  • Tap the stock pile to draw new cards.
  • Undo reverses your last move, one step at a time.
  • Auto Move sends any currently eligible top cards to their foundations — it won't play the rest of the game for you, and you can still pull cards back off a foundation onto the tableau.
