How to Play

Klondike Solitaire
  • Drag a card (or a valid stack) onto a foundation or another tableau pile.
  • Double-tap a card to send it straight to its foundation if possible.
  • Tap the stock pile to draw new cards.
  • An empty tableau column only accepts a King.
  • Undo reverses your last move. Hint highlights one move you can currently make. Auto Move sends any currently eligible top cards to their foundations.
