How to Play

Yukon Solitaire
  • All 52 cards are dealt out at the start — there's no stock or waste pile.
  • Any face-up card can be dragged together with every card stacked below it, even if that sub-stack isn't in order.
  • A card (or group) can only be dropped where the top card of the destination is the opposite color and one rank higher — same as Klondike.
  • An empty tableau column only accepts a King (or a group led by one).
  • Undo reverses your last move. Hint highlights one move you can currently make. Auto Move sends any currently eligible top cards to their foundations.
