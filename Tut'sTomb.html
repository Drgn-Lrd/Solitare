How to Play

Tut's Tomb (Pyramid)
  • Select two exposed cards that add up to 13 to remove them.
  • Valid pairs: 10+3, J+2, Q+A, etc.
  • Kings are worth 13 and can be removed by themselves with a single tap.
  • Tap a selected card again to deselect it.
  • Tap the draw pile to cycle through cards. Relaxed Rules: you can cycle the deck indefinitely.
  • Clear a full pyramid row to send the adventurer past an obstacle; clear the whole pyramid to reach the chest and claim the treasure!
