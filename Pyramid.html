How to Play

Tut's Tomb (Pyramid)
  • Select two exposed cards that add up to 13 to remove them.
  • Valid pairs: 10+3, J+2, Q+A, etc.
  • Kings are worth 13 and can be removed by themselves with a single tap.
  • Tap the draw pile to cycle through cards. Relaxed Rules: You can cycle the deck infinitely.
  • Clear the entire pyramid to win the game and claim the treasure!
