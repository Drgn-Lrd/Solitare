How to Play

FreeCell Solitaire
  • All 52 cards are dealt face-up — there's no hidden information, every FreeCell game is (in principle) solvable.
  • Build tableau columns DOWN in alternating color (like Klondike), but unlike Klondike, an empty column accepts any card, not just a King.
  • The 4 free cells (top-left) can each hold exactly one card of any rank/suit, as a temporary parking spot.
  • Drag a card (or a valid run) onto a foundation, another tableau pile, or a free cell — or click a card, then click a destination.
  • You can move several cards at once ("supermove") as long as you have enough free cells + empty columns to support it — the highlight will only show a destination as valid if the whole move is legal.
  • Undo reverses your last move. Hint highlights one move you can currently make. Auto Move sends any currently eligible top cards to their foundations.
